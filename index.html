We are Bauan's premiere casual dining restaurant

Everything just started as a dream of giving Bauan a chance to shine on the gastronomical map of Batangas. We open each day trying to reach out to locals and tourists alike to share our modern take to Batangas cuisine.

Your Primary Stop-over to Premiere Diving Spots

Situated along the Bauan-Batangas provincial road, treat yourself to a stop-over that you truly deserve. Take a break at Bauan, Batangas' central gateway to world famous dive spots and cultural heritage towns and be rewarded with modern treats you'll surely enjoy.

Live, Love, Laugh

Spend memorable and intimate dining experiences with us and you'll never regret it. We have designed our space to make you feel always at home, whether when you're with closest friends, colleagues and family.

Cajun Inspired Roasting

We use our own secretly formulated cajun-inspired mix on roasted recipes. Chicken has never been this good in this side of the province. Paired with mashed potatoes and mixed vegetables, you're sure in for an amazing treat whether its lunch or dinner.

Modern Batangas Cuisine at its Finest

Batangas has a rich gastronomical heritage. The province prides itself with exceptional produce, and amazing meats which produced authentic local recipes to be handed over from one generation to the next. Try our tributes to local cuisine and you'll realize how blessed the province is with its culture of food. Try our Adobong Bulalo and Cajun-inspired baby back ribs today!

Bauan's Egg-wrapped Mixed Noodles

A true local favorite, the humble Bauan pansit will surely fulfill your appetite. Pair it with our amazing freshly-squeezed honey mint lemonade today!
